Guild Gaming: Building a community-driven gaming platform

Client: Guild Gaming
Product Sector: Gaming, Community
Team: Solo UX Designer
Duration: 3 months
Deliverables: Research, Wireframes, Prototypes

Overview

Guild Gaming is a platform designed to connect gamers, facilitate team formation, and create a thriving community around competitive gaming. The project focused on creating an intuitive interface that makes it easy for players to find teammates, join tournaments, and track their gaming progress.

The Problem

Competitive gamers often struggle to find reliable teammates, coordinate schedules, and track their performance across different games. Existing solutions are fragmented and don't provide a comprehensive platform for serious gamers to build their competitive careers.

The Solution

A unified platform that combines team discovery, tournament management, and performance tracking. The solution focuses on creating seamless user experiences that help gamers focus on what matters most - improving their skills and building lasting partnerships.

Process

Research
Conducted user interviews and competitive analysis to understand gamer needs and pain points.
Design
Created wireframes and prototypes focusing on intuitive navigation and clear information hierarchy.

Key Features

Team Discovery
Find compatible teammates based on skill level, game preferences, and availability.
Tournament Hub
Browse and join tournaments with integrated registration and bracket management.
Performance Tracking
Monitor your progress across different games and tournaments with detailed analytics.
Community Features
Build your gaming network with forums, friend systems, and team management tools.
CONCLUSION

Key Learnings

This project reinforced the importance of understanding user workflows in competitive environments. Gamers need quick access to information and seamless interactions to maintain their focus during gameplay. The design process highlighted how community features can significantly enhance user engagement and platform stickiness.
