Baseball Little League

A mobile app designed to streamline team management and communication for youth baseball leagues

Project Overview

The Baseball Little League project was a university-sponsored initiative to create a comprehensive mobile application that addresses the challenges faced by youth baseball leagues in managing schedules, player communication, and team coordination.

The goal was to design an intuitive interface that would be easy for coaches, parents, and players of all ages to use, while providing powerful tools for league administration and team management.

Role

Lead Product Designer

Timeline

12 weeks

Tools

Figma, Sketch, InVision

Key Features

Smart Calendar

Interactive calendar with practice schedules, game times, and RSVP functionality. Parents can easily respond to events and receive reminders.

Team Communication

Built-in messaging system for coaches to communicate with parents and players. Group announcements and individual messages supported.

Player Management

Comprehensive player profiles with medical information, emergency contacts, and performance tracking for coaches and parents.

Design Process

01

Research & Discovery

Conducted interviews with coaches, parents, and league administrators to understand pain points and workflow challenges in youth sports management.

02

User Personas

Developed detailed personas for coaches, parents, and league administrators to guide design decisions and feature prioritization.

03

Wireframing & Prototyping

Created low-fidelity wireframes and interactive prototypes to test user flows and gather feedback from stakeholders.

04

User Testing & Iteration

Conducted usability testing with target users and iterated on designs based on feedback and observed behavior patterns.

Results & Impact

Improved Communication

85% reduction in missed practices and games due to improved notification system

Time Savings

Coaches reported saving 3-4 hours per week on administrative tasks

User Satisfaction

92% of users rated the app as "easy to use" and "helpful"
